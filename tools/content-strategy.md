# BuyAndScrap.com — Content Strategy
## Based on HubSpot Pillar/Cluster Model

---

## Core SEO Concepts Applied

### 1. Pillar Pages (Topic Authority)
Broad, comprehensive pages that cover a main topic in depth.
Each pillar targets a high-volume head keyword and links to all cluster content beneath it.
Google sees the pillar as the authority hub for that topic.

### 2. Cluster Content (Long-tail Keywords)
Specific articles around subtopics of the pillar.
Each links back to the pillar page + related clusters.
Long-tail = lower competition, higher buyer intent, easier to rank.

### 3. User Intent (4 Types)
- **Informational:** "how to...", "what is...", "why..." — early stage, build trust
- **Navigational:** branded searches, tool searches — middle stage
- **Commercial investigation:** "best...", "cheapest...", "compare..." — nearly ready to buy
- **Transactional:** "buy cheap car Manchester", "scrap car near me" — ready to act NOW

### 4. Internal Linking Strategy
- Every cluster article links to its pillar (with exact-match anchor text)
- Every pillar links to all its clusters
- Location pages link to relevant listings
- Blog posts link to /sell and /cars CTAs
- MOT checker links to relevant listing pages
